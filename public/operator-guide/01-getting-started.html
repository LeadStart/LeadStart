Getting Started

Where you log in, what each piece of the dashboard is for, and what your role lets you do. Read this once before you touch anything else.

Step 1 Open the login page

1

Go to the production URL

Open your browser to https://leadstart-ebon.vercel.app/app/login. That's the only URL you need to bookmark.

The /app at the end is required — it's the app's base path. If you go to the bare domain you'll see a 404. (For local development the URL is http://localhost:3000/app/login.)

Step 2 Sign in with your operator account

2

Use the email + password the owner gave you

LeadStart uses Supabase Auth — the same email and password you'd use anywhere. There is no SSO and no magic link in this version.

  • Enter email, enter password, click Sign In.
  • If your role is owner or va, you'll land on /app/admin — the operator dashboard.
  • If your role is client, you'll land on /app/client — the client portal (a different view; see Step 6 for what each role sees).
Watch out — wrong landing page?

If you land on /app/client when you expected to be in the admin view, your role isn't set to owner or va in Supabase. Ask the owner to update your profiles.role column. It's not something you can fix from the UI.

Step 5 The top bar — what each control does

5

Three things you'll actually use

The top bar is intentionally minimal. Most actions live on the page itself, not up here.

  • Search — placeholder for now. Doesn't return live results yet. Don't rely on it.
  • Notification bell — placeholder. Hot‑lead alerts go to email, not the bell. (See Guide 04.)
  • Your name + avatar — opens a dropdown with Profile, Settings, Switch view (only if you have multiple roles), and Sign out.
Heads up

The bell with the red dot is a placeholder. It does not indicate unread hot leads. The way you actually find new replies is the Inbox sidebar item or the email Resend sends to the client.

Step 6 Roles — who can do what

6

Three roles, two dashboards

Your role is set in Supabase (profiles.role) and determines which dashboard you land on after login. The middleware enforces this on every request, so there's no way to "sneak in" by typing a URL.

Role Sees admin (/admin)? Sees client portal (/client)? Can do
owner Yes No (own org only) Everything: clients, campaigns, billing, team, API keys, webhook registration.
va Yes No Most operator tasks. Cannot manage team or API keys.
client No Yes (their data only) View their own campaigns, reports, feedback, hot‑lead inbox. Submit feedback.

Heads up Common gotchas

You're on production

The URL to bookmark is https://leadstart-ebon.vercel.app/app/login. Anything you do there is live for paying clients. There is no separate "staging" or "demo" mode.

Search and notifications are placeholders

The search bar at the top doesn't return real results yet. The bell is decorative. Don't expect either to do anything — use the sidebar.

Forgot your password?

The "Forgot your password?" link sends a Supabase reset email. Check spam. If you don't get it within a few minutes, ping the owner — your account may not exist in Supabase yet.
